Branching Narratives:

Video Games' Evolution of Storytelling

Artist's Statement

In the evolving landscape of storytelling, a revolution unfolds within the dynamic realm of video games. For decades, this transformative era has been marked by interactive experiences that redefine the conventional boundaries of traditional media forms, transcending the linear constraints of cinema. It is within this context that I found the creation of a webpage to be the most effective medium for expressing my argument, much like the immersive narratives found in video games.

My decision to utilize a website for my argument stems from my desire to utilize its multifaceted and interactive nature, like video games. In a similar way to video games’ integration of visuals, audio, and player agency, a webpage allows for an incorporation of videos, images, audio, and interactive features. This fusion of elements creates an immersive experience surpassing the limitations of essays and articles.

The concept of my website began with the construction of my argument. Video games are something I feel strongly about in their power as storytelling tools, and I find them unlike any other medium. Thanks to this, the argument I would make became clear. Following the construction of my argument, I planned the layout of my site. I wanted to create a layout that facilitated a linear flow and engaged readers. These factors lead me to create a next and previous navigation button to navigate the different pages. Utilizing HTML, CSS, and minor JavaScript, I created a simple layout, allowing multimedia elements and writing to speak for themselves. I worked to make the design responsive and able to be read on a breadth of screen sizes, expanding the reach of my argument. This approach utilizes the versatility of markup languages and the reach of the internet.

In addition to these goals, I attempted to improve upon my last implementation of a webpage for project #3. I used the same multimedia aspects to engage viewers with the addition of a quiz aspect, with the goal of making readers think further about the topics I cover. Also, I introduced visitors to the layout of the page and what the goal of the site is on the home page.

In the same way video games redefine storytelling, webpages challenge traditional communication mediums. The integration of multimedia elements, coupled with interactive design, transforms my argument into an immersive experience. My hope is that this project sparks conversation and the recognition of video games as an unparalleled unique storytelling tool thanks to the mediums engaging properties.
