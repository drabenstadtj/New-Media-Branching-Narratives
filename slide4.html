Branching Narratives:

Video Games' Evolution of Storytelling

Conclusions

Video games have undergone a transformation in terms of their storytelling marked by branching narratives, player agency, and dynamic storytelling. This transformation has challenged established norms of traditional media forms such as movies, television, and literature. Video games have historically been dismissed as simply entertainment; however, they utilize a narrative depth that defies these notions.

The heart of video game storytelling lies in the hands of players, where choices made become pivotal in shaping the unfolding narratives. This interactivity and agency elevate storytelling to a participatory and dynamic experience, challenging the linear structure of cinematic storytelling. As players grapple with decisions that send ripples through the following storyline, the complexity inherent in video game narratives becomes apparent, introducing unpredictability and replayability.

Furthermore, the collaborative nature of video game storytelling emerges as a crucial aspect of the medium. Game designers craft scenarios where player choices appear to lead to consequences, fostering agency. However, within this, the narrative has been designed to pivot by game creators, creating dynamic and unexpected turns of events. This manipulation of player perception reveals a collaboration between players and creators, where both are actively contributing to the narrative experience.

The case study of Until Dawn exemplifies this collaborative venture, where creators craft a narrative that toys with expectations, and players, in turn, contribute through emotional investment and interpretations. Through various video game narratives, this collaboration proves an integral part of the medium as it evolves, highlighting its malleability in storytelling. The narrative can change in unexpected directions, unveiling a shared journey where creators and players actively shape the storytelling experience.

Video games redefine the storytelling landscape, offering a unique and collaborative form of narrative art that surpasses the linear constraints of cinema as a medium. As games evolve, the boundaries of authorship blur, and storytelling becomes a shared venture between the creators and players, forging a new path in the art of narratives.
