Artist's Statment

Creating a website provided the best platform for expressing my argument due to its ability to reach wide audiences, integrate multimedia elements, and offer an interactive experience. The decision to use a website stemmed from the idea of supplementing my relatively writing-heavy project with media elements.

The multifaceted and digital nature of blockchain deemed the use of a medium that combines visual and auditory elements. Websites allow for integration of videos, images, audio clips, and interactive features, enabling an immersive experience. In contrast to an essay, a website offers a familiar and pertinent space to allow viewers to engage with my arguments.

The creation of my website involved a process beginning with conceptualizing my idea, outlining the concepts into writing, and supplementing the concepts with multimedia elements. To maintain a cohesive narrative, I organized my content into sections flowing from one to the next. Using HTML, CSS, and minimal JavaScript I created a visually engaging layout that enhances my writing. In addition, I used responsive design to ensure accessibility on nearly all devices which increases the reach and inclusivity.

The aim of my project is to spread awareness about the environmental impact of blockchain technology, especially ones using Proof of Work consensus systems. By using a platform that transcends geographical boundaries, I hope to reach and inform many viewers across the world and inspire conversation and discussion about blockchain and new technologies in general.

The decision to express my ideas through a website comes from a desire to embrace the versatility and accessibility of the internet and web design. The process involved a fusion of conceptualization, design, coding, and writing, resulting in an immersive online experience. I hope my project sparks meaningful conversation, resonates with a wide audience, and contributes to broader discussion on the environmental impacts of blockchain technology.
